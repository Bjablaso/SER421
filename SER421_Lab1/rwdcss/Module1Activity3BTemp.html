Kevin A. Gary, Ph.D.

Welcome to my homepage! I have been on the faculty at ASU since 2004, tenured in 2010. I believe in creating challenging learning experiences for my students that prepares them for industry, the profession, and life! I do this through hands-on, project-based teaching in the Software Enterprise, use-inspired applied research (particularly in e-Learning and healthcare), and applying industry best practices in the classroom. My primary area is "Software Engineering", but my interests vary from enterprise computing to mHealth to agile and open source software engineering.
